The Best Movies According To Uddipta

My Top Three Movies Of All-Time

Good Will Hunting

I am drawn to stories about hidden potential, fear of failure, emotional vulnerability, and figuring out my future while feeling stuck. Will’s mix of intelligence, avoidance, insecurity, and emotional walls matches a lot of the themes that I always think about.

500 Days Of Summer

I connect strongly with emotional attachment, idealization, nostalgia, and “what could’ve been” relationships. This movie has that lonely romantic introspection I tend to resonate with.

Shawshank Redemption

Because underneath all my anxiety about the future, career, family, and life direction, there’s still this recurring desire for freedom, dignity, stability, and eventually “making it out.” I seem to like hopeful stories where endurance matters.
